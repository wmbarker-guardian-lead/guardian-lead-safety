Process FAQs

Common questions about our inspection process and what to expect.

How long does an inspection take?

Most visits last 1–2 hours depending on the size of the property and number of units.

Do tenants need to leave?

No. Tenants can remain, though children and pets should be kept away during sampling.

When will I get results?

Typical turnaround is 7–10 days, but rush options are available at additional cost.

What if hazards are found?

You’ll need to hire an RRP-certified contractor or a licensed abatement firm to address hazards. Financial assistance programs may help cover costs.

Do you handle remediation?

No, we provide inspection and reporting only. We can refer you to qualified contractors.

Do I need a clearance exam after work?

Yes, a clearance exam is required after lead hazard control or abatement work to ensure the property is safe for occupancy.
