Prep Checklist

Follow these steps to prepare your property for a smooth inspection process.

  • Clear floors and window sills of toys, plants, and personal belongings.
  • Ensure rooms are accessible for inspection and sampling.
  • Do not clean or disturb dust for at least 24 hours prior to the visit.
  • Secure pets away from work areas during the appointment.
  • Provide access to basements, attics, or exterior areas if applicable.

Proper preparation helps ensure accurate results and faster reporting.
