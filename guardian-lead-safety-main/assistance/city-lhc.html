City of Cleveland Lead Hazard Control (LHC) Grants

Cleveland’s Lead Hazard Control program provides grants to assist property owners in addressing lead-based paint hazards, with a priority for homes with young children.

Who Can Apply

  • Property owners with homes built before 1978
  • Properties housing children under six
  • Owner-occupied and rental units within Cleveland city limits

What’s Covered

Funding supports paint stabilization, interim controls, and other hazard reduction work.

How to Apply

Applications are processed by the City’s Department of Community Development.
