Prep Checklist

Preparing for your inspection helps us work efficiently and reduces the need for return visits. Here’s how you can make sure your property is ready for the inspection.

Before We Arrive

  • Provide access to all rooms and areas
  • Clear windowsills, floors, and surfaces where dust wipes will be collected
  • Make sure children and pets are not present during sampling

What to Expect

  • Inspection typically takes 1–2 hours
  • We’ll collect dust wipes and may collect paint-chip/soil samples if applicable
  • Minimal disruption — we respect your property

Questions?

We’re happy to walk you through the process before we arrive.
