Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take?

Site visit 1–2 hrs; reports ~7–10 days. Rush lab options available.

What’s tested?

Painted surfaces, dust wipes; soil and paint-chip if applicable.

RRP vs Abatement?

RRP manages hazards during renovation; abatement permanently removes hazards (used for the 20-year pathway).

Multi-unit buildings?

We design compliant, efficient sampling strategies.
