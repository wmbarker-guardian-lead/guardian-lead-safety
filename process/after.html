After the Visit

Once your inspection is complete, here’s what happens next:

Lab Analysis

Samples are sent to a certified lab. Results are typically available within 5–7 business days.

Your Report

You’ll receive a detailed report outlining results, risk levels, and any required follow-up actions.

Next Steps

  • Review recommendations in your report
  • Apply for financial assistance if remediation is required
  • Schedule a clearance exam (if applicable)
