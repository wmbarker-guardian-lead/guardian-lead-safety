Inspection Timeline

Here’s what to expect during the inspection and reporting process. Times may vary depending on lab processing and shipping speed.

Step 1: Schedule

Request online or by phone. We’ll confirm availability and answer questions about your property.

Step 2: Site Visit

Average length: 1–2 hours depending on unit size and number of samples.

Step 3: Sample Shipment

Samples are shipped to an accredited lab, usually arriving within 1–3 business days.

Step 4: Lab Processing

Standard turnaround ≈ 5 business days. Rush options available (3-day, 1-day, or same-day).

Step 5: Report Issued

Final report delivered ≈ 7–10 days after site visit. Rush processing shortens turnaround.
