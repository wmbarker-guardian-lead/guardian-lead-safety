FAQs

Are child-care centers required to test for lead?
In Ohio, licensed facilities must meet lead-safe requirements, and inspections are often required for certification and licensing.
How often should schools test?
Frequency depends on state and local regulations, but annual or bi-annual testing is recommended for facilities serving young children.
Do you coordinate with licensing agencies?
Yes. We provide documentation and reports formatted for use with ODH and local regulators.
