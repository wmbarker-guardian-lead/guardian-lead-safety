Lead Clearance Exam

Post-renovation, interim control, or abatement verification. HUD/EPA compliant clearance exams ensure your property is safe for re-occupancy.

Schedule an Exam ODH Requirements

When Clearance is Required

  • After renovation or repair under EPA RRP Rule
  • Following interim controls (repairs, painting, cleaning)
  • Upon completion of lead hazard abatement projects
  • Before re-occupancy of treated areas

Exam Process

  • Visual inspection for dust/debris and paint condition
  • Collection of HUD/EPA dust wipe samples from floors, sills, wells
  • Accredited lab analysis with strict chain of custody
  • Clearance report with pass/fail determination
