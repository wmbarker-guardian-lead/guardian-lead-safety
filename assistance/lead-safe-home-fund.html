Lead Safe Home Fund (Cleveland)

Program Summary

Assistance for Cleveland pre-1978 housing: grants and per-unit incentives ($750 certification / $1,000 20-year exemption).

Eligibility

  • In Cleveland
  • Pre-1978
  • Rental or owner-occupied
  • Assessment/clearance completed

How It Works

  1. Assessment/clearance with Guardian
  2. Gather docs
  3. Apply via Coalition portal
  4. Licensed RRP/abatement work
  5. Final clearance → incentives

Pitfalls

  • Missing parcel/tax docs
  • Unlicensed contractor
  • Late submission
