Daycare-Specific Support

Daycare centers face stricter compliance requirements around lead safety. Funding programs often prioritize facilities serving children under six.

Who Qualifies

  • Licensed daycare centers
  • Facilities in pre-1978 buildings
  • Both non-profits and private operators

Funding Opportunities

Grants, low-interest loans, and cost-sharing options are available for lead hazard control in daycare settings.
