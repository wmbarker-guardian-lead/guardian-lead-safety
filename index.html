How It Works

1. Schedule Visit

Book online or by phone. Most appointments within a week.

2. On-Site Assessment

Our ODH-licensed inspector collects samples and documents conditions.

3. Lab Testing

Certified laboratory analyzes dust and paint samples for lead content.

4. Report & Guidance

You receive a full report with recommendations for compliance or clearance.

Report issued ≈ 7–10 days after visit. Rush options available.
